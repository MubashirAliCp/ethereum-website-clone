What is Ethereum?

Ethereum is a technology that's home to digital
money, global payments, and applications. The community has built a booming digital economy, bold new ways for creators to earn online, and so much more. It's open to everyone, wherever you are in the world – all you need is the internet.

A fairer financial system

Today, billions of people can’t open bank
accounts, others have their payments blocked.
Ethereum's decentralized finance (DeFi) system
never sleeps or discriminates. With just an
internet connection, you can send, receive,
borrow, earn interest, and even stream funds
anywhere in the world.

An open internet

Today, we gain access to 'free' internet services
by giving up control of our personal data.
Ethereum services are open by default – you
just need a wallet. These are free and easy to
set up, controlled by you, and work without any
personal info.
